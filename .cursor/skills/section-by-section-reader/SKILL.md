# Section-by-Section Reader

Per section output:

- section_objective
- main_claim (**explicit** | **inferred**)
- important_concepts
- definitions
- key_evidence (with page/line ref)
- limitations stated by authors
- open_questions
- relationship_to_other_sections

## Rules

- **source_text**: short excerpt with ref
- **summary**: your compression
- **inference**: must be labeled
- **uncertainty**: when OCR/conversion quality low
