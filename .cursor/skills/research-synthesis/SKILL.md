# Research Synthesis

## Buckets

- explicit_claims (single paper, quoted/summary with ref)
- supported_by_multiple (list papers)
- disputed_claims
- methodological_differences
- open_research_questions
- model_inferences (clearly labeled)

Never merge inference into explicit_claims.
